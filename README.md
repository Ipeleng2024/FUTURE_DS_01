# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Superstore sales data using Excel and Power BI to uncover business insights related to sales performance, profitability, product categories, and regional trends.

The dashboard was designed to help stakeholders understand key business metrics and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objective
The objective of this project is to:

- Analyze sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Compare regional business performance
- Discover sales trends over time
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## Tools Used

- Excel
- Power BI
- GitHub

---

## Dataset

Dataset used:
Superstore Sales Dataset

The dataset includes:
- Sales
- Profit
- Product Categories
- Regions
- Customer Information
- Order Dates

---

## Data Cleaning Process

The dataset was cleaned in Excel before importing into Power BI.

Cleaning steps included:
- Removing duplicate rows
- Fixing date formatting issues
- Checking for missing values
- Formatting numeric columns
- Organizing relevant business fields

---

## KPIs

The following KPIs were created in Power BI:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value

---

## Key Insights

- Technology products generated the highest overall sales revenue.
- Furniture products showed strong sales but lower profitability.
- The West region consistently outperformed other regions.
- Sales increased significantly during Q4, indicating seasonal demand.
- Some products generated high sales volumes but lower profit margins.

---

## Recommendations

- Increase marketing efforts for high-profit product categories.
- Improve performance strategies in lower-performing regions.
- Focus inventory planning on top-selling products.
- Introduce seasonal campaigns during peak sales periods.
- Review pricing and discount strategies for low-profit products.
